Seogwipo, the city on the volcanic coast

Why Seogwipo?

Seogwipo is known for its delicious cuisines in its coastal town. As it takes you across the verdant forests and dramatic volcanic landscapes. It is home to three awe inspiring waterfalls, plus a stunning coastline filled with rocky coves and bathing pools.

The Seogwipo coast is always busy with fishing boats and is adorned with majestic trees. The inner harbor is a favored location for capturing sea bream and groupers. You have the opportunity to embark on a daytime open-sea fishing excursion by boarding a boat from the port.
